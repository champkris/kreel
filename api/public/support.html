Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the Kreels app from the App Store or Google Play. Tap "Sign Up" and follow the registration process. You can sign up with your email or use Google/Apple sign-in.

How do I watch content?

Browse the Home screen to discover trending content, or use the Explore tab to search for specific dramas, series, or creators. Tap on any content to start watching.

What is the difference between free and premium content?

Free content is available to all users. Premium content requires a subscription or one-time purchase. Look for the "Free," "Premium," or "Exclusive" badges on content.

How do I send gifts during live streams?

First, add coins to your wallet through the Profile > Wallet section. During a live stream, tap the gift icon to select and send virtual gifts to the creator.

How do I become a creator?

Go to Profile and select "Upgrade to Pro" to become a professional creator. You'll need to complete verification before you can upload content and monetize.

How do I join or create a club?

Navigate to the Club tab. To join a club, browse available clubs and tap "Join." To create your own club, tap the "+" button and fill in your club details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Settings > Account Settings > Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
